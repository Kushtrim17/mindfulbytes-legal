Frequently Asked Questions

What is MindfulBytes?

MindfulBytes is a screen time companion app that helps you build healthier digital and physical habits by setting health goals, monitoring social media usage, and rewarding mindful choices.

How do rewards work?

Set your daily health goals and decide how much time you want to spend on distracting apps as a reward. MindfulBytes connects with Apple Health to track your progress, and once you achieve your goals, your reward time is unlocked.

Can I use MindfulBytes without a subscription?

Yes. You can use MindfulBytes for free but once you set your goals, distracting apps and rewards you cannot change them.

What if I feel the urge to just use my social media apps?

We don’t judge — our goal is to help you understand your triggers. You can tap the “Give me a cheap dopamine” button to get a 15-minute break. You’ll also specify why you want this break, for example: Stress, Anxiety, Depression, Urgency, or other reasons. This helps you track patterns and become more mindful over time.

Does MindfulBytes collect my personal data?

We value your privacy. MindfulBytes does not collect personally identifiable information. We do use Firebase Analytics to anonymously collect app usage data, which helps us understand how users interact with the app and improve their experience.
