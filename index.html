What happens if I unblock social media apps?

MindfulBytes is free as long as you stick to your goals. If you manually unblock social media apps, you may need to subscribe to continue using the app.

Can I use MindfulBytes without a subscription?

Yes. You can use MindfulBytes for free if you avoid unblocking distracting apps. Subscription is only required if you choose to bypass the system frequently.

Does MindfulBytes collect my personal data?

No. Your app usage data stays private and is only stored on your device. We value your privacy and do not sell or share personal information.

How do I reset my goals?

You can update or reset your goals anytime from the Dashboard. Simply adjust your parameters and save your new goals.
